INDUMATI



Description: Princess Indumati is the daughter of King Indraverma and a close friend of Bheem. She is admired for her intelligence and strong will.

Personality: Brave, intelligent, and compassionate. Indumati cares deeply for her kingdom and often assists Bheem and his friends in their adventures.

Characteristics: Indumati typically wears a traditional royal attire with vibrant colors and jewelry, reflecting her status as a princess.
