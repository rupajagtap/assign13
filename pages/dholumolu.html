DHOLU MOLU



Description: Dholu and Bholu are twin brothers and Kalia's sidekicks, known for their loyalty to him and their playful antics.

Personality: Cheerful, mischievous, and easily excitable. Dholu and Bholu enjoy causing trouble but are ultimately good-hearted.

Characteristics: Both wear identical outfits: red dhotis with yellow vests. They often follow Kalia's lead and provide comic relief in the series.
