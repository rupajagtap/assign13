BHEEM



Description: The main protagonist, Bheem, is a young boy known for his exceptional strength, bravery, and kindness. He is always ready to help his friends and protect Dholakpur from any threats.

Personality: Courageous, loyal, and compassionate. He believes in fairness and justice.

Characteristics: Bheem is often depicted wearing a yellow dhoti with a red vest and a jeweled belt. He is renowned for his ability to defeat enemies and solve problems using his physical strength and intelligence.
