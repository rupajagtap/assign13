KALIA



Description: Kalia is initially portrayed as Bheem's rival but later becomes one of his friends. He is known for his strength and competitive spirit.

Personality: Boastful, confident, and sometimes stubborn. Kalia values his friendship with Bheem and the others despite their occasional disagreements.

Characteristics:Kalia is depicted as a bulky boy wearing a blue dhoti and a green vest. He often teams up with Dholu and Bholu, creating humorous situations.
